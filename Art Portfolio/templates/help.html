Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" link in the navigation menu, fill out the required information, and submit the form.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" link in the navigation menu and then select "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password.

Troubleshooting Tips

Issue: Unable to log in

Make sure you are entering the correct username and password. If you have forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

Issue: Website not loading properly

Clear your browser's cache and cookies, and ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try accessing the website from a different browser or device.

Issue: Problems with uploading artwork

Ensure that the file size and format of the artwork meet our upload requirements. If you continue to experience issues, contact our support team for assistance.
